Kobe Bryant: The Black Mamba

Kobe Bean Bryant was born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He was the son of former NBA player Joe "Jellybean" Bryant and grew up immersed in basketball culture, spending part of his youth in Italy where his father played professionally.

Kobe entered the NBA directly from high school in 1996 at the age of 17, selected by the Charlotte Hornets and quickly traded to the Los Angeles Lakers. He would go on to play his entire 20-year career with the Lakers, becoming an 18-time All-Star and 5-time NBA Champion. His incredible scoring ability peaked with an 81-point game in 2006, the second highest in NBA history.
